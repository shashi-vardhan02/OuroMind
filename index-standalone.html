FAQ

How accurate is the patient simulation?
−
Our models are trained on thousands of anonymized clinical encounters, making their resistance patterns, deflection techniques, and emotional responses highly realistic.
Can I use OuroMind for CEUs?
+
Yes, our Pro and Clinic tiers include certified modules that are approved for CEUs by major psychological associations.
What modalities does the report cover?
+
Currently, our reports analyze your interventions through the lenses of CBT, DBT, ACT, and Psychodynamic therapy, tagging your approaches automatically.
